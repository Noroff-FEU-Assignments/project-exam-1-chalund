About

"Life Above the Arctic Circle" is a blog that explores everyday life in northern Norway. It covers the beauty of each season, from enchanting mountain hikes to the joys and challenges of daily living in the region.

Who am I

Charlotte 35 years old, lives on Sortland in Vesterålen with my partner and our beloved dog Hjørdis.

Originally from Lyngen, a place further north, the coincidences make me end up here.

Join me in embracing the beauty and charm of life above the Arctic Circle as I share my experiences in this remarkable region..
